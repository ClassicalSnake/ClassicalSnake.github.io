CLASSICAL SNAKE

SCORE: 0

Classical Snake is basic snake video game. The objective is to control the snake to make it eat has much food items as possible without making the snake hitting itself. The more food items eaten the more the user scores. Classical Snake is available free of charge and without advertisements on desktop devices.

Unfortunately Classical Snake does not work on mobile.
